Painting

Interior and Exterior Painting Services

Exterior Painting: Protect & Enhance Your Home

Your home’s exterior is the first thing people see — and the first line of defense against weather. Our exterior painting services not only boost curb appeal but also provide essential protection against moisture, sun, and temperature changes. We begin every project with thorough surface preparation — power washing, scraping, and priming — to ensure maximum paint adhesion and durability. We use high-performance paints and coatings designed to withstand harsh weather while maintaining their color and finish for years. From siding and trim to doors, garages, and shutters, every surface is treated with care. With Kenna Roofing, you can count on a beautiful, professional finish that adds value and shields your home from the elements.

Interior Painting: Transform Your Living Space

A quality interior paint job does more than refresh a room — it creates an inviting, personalized atmosphere for your family and guests. Our interior painting experts take the time to protect your furniture and flooring, repair minor imperfections, and apply clean, even coats for a flawless look. Whether it’s one room or your entire home, we work with your vision to bring it to life — using low-VOC, high-quality paints that are safe, long-lasting, and beautifully smooth. From walls and ceilings to baseboards, cabinets, and accents, we’ll transform your space with precision and care, leaving you with a finish you’ll love for years to come.

Why Choose Professional Painting?

Professional painting offers more than aesthetics — it’s an investment in your home’s protection, comfort, and long-term value. Here’s why homeowners trust Kenna Roofing for their painting needs:

  • Superior Quality: We use top-grade paints and materials for long-lasting beauty.
  • Proper Preparation: Every surface is cleaned, primed, and prepped for lasting results.
  • Expert Application: Our painters deliver smooth, even finishes with meticulous attention to detail.
  • Time & Cost Efficiency: Save time, avoid stress, and ensure your project is done right the first time.
  • Enhanced Home Value: A professional paint job can significantly increase curb appeal and resale value.

Comprehensive Gutter Services

Professional Gutter Installation & Replacement

A properly installed gutter system does more than just move water — it prevents long-term damage to your roof and property. Our team specializes in seamless gutter installation, ensuring a precise fit and leak-free performance. We use high-quality aluminum and steel materials that resist rust, corrosion, and warping, so your gutters stand up to harsh weather. Whether you’re building a new home or replacing worn-out gutters, we tailor every system to fit your home’s exact needs and style.

Benefits of Gutter Maintenance

Routine gutter cleaning and maintenance protect your home’s most important structural elements. Neglected gutters can lead to water overflow, foundation cracks, roof leaks, and even mold growth inside your walls. Regular upkeep helps you:

  • Prevent Water Damage: Keep water flowing away from your home to avoid costly repairs.
  • Protect Your Roof & Foundation: Reduce the risk of roof rot and basement flooding.
  • Extend Roof Life: Reduce standing water and ice buildup that can damage shingles.
  • Improve Curb Appeal: Clean, well-maintained gutters enhance your home’s exterior.
  • Avoid Pest Infestations: Eliminate stagnant water that attracts insects and rodents.

Our team offers scheduled cleaning and inspection services to ensure your gutters work efficiently year-round.

Why Invest In New Gutters?

If your gutters are sagging, leaking, or overflowing during rain, it may be time for a replacement. Modern gutter systems not only perform better but also improve your home’s appearance and value. With seamless designs, protective guards, and durable finishes, Kenna Roofing’s gutter solutions offer:

  • Leak-Free Protection: Seamless construction minimizes weak points and leaks.
  • Low Maintenance: Gutter guards reduce clogs and keep water flowing smoothly.
  • Long-Term Durability: Built to last through heavy rain, snow, and wind.
  • Custom Fit & Finish: Designed to match your roofline and complement your home.

Investing in new gutters today can prevent thousands in potential damage tomorrow. With expert installation, high-quality materials, and dependable service, Kenna Roofing delivers results you can count on — protecting your home from the top down.

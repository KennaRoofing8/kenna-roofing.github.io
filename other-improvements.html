Home Improvement Experts

Wide-Array of Home Improvement Capabilties

At Kenna Roofing, our expertise extends far beyond roofs. With over 15 years of experience in construction and home renovation, we proudly offer a range of home improvement services designed to enhance both the beauty and functionality of your home — inside and out. Whether you’re updating interiors, upgrading exterior features, or restoring your property after years of wear, our skilled team delivers dependable craftsmanship and results you can trust. When you choose Kenna Roofing, you’re not just hiring contractors — you’re partnering with a team that truly values your satisfaction. We combine decades of hands-on experience with a commitment to honesty, communication, and craftsmanship.

Exterior Enhancements

Your home’s exterior makes the first impression — and Kenna Roofing helps you make it a lasting one. Every project is built with premium materials and precise attention to detail to ensure your home looks its best and stands strong against the elements. We offer a wide range of exterior improvements to boost curb appeal and long-term durability, including:

  • Deck and Porch Construction: Custom-built spaces for relaxing, entertaining, or enjoying the outdoors.
  • Awnings and Shade Structures: Stylish, weather-resistant solutions for comfort and protection.
  • Fascia and Soffit Repairs: Keeping your roofline strong, ventilated, and attractive.
  • Window and Door Replacement: Improving energy efficiency and security with quality installations.

Interior Renovations

Kenna Roofing isn’t just an exterior expert — we also bring our dedication to quality craftsmanship inside your home. From small updates to full remodels, we help you create comfortable, functional, and modern spaces that reflect your style.

  • Drywall and Ceiling Repair: Smoothing out damage and restoring clean, professional finishes.
  • Flooring Installation: Durable, stylish options for every room — from hardwood to tile and vinyl.
  • Interior Painting and Trim Work: Fresh colors and finishes that elevate your living spaces.
  • Basement and Attic Renovations: Turning unused areas into valuable, livable space.

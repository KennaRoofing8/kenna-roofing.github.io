Siding

High-Quality Siding Services

Professional Siding Installation & Replacement

New siding is one of the most effective ways to refresh your home’s exterior and improve its overall performance. Your home’s siding does more than enhance its appearance — it provides critical protection from the elements and helps maintain energy efficiency year-round. Whether you’re upgrading old, faded panels or starting from scratch, Kenna Roofing delivers top-tier installation backed by years of hands-on experience. We offer a variety of materials — including vinyl, fiber cement, wood, and composite siding — each selected for its strength, appearance, and longevity. Our team ensures every panel is properly aligned, sealed, and secured for lasting protection against wind, rain, and temperature changes. With expert installation and durable materials, your home will look sharp and stay protected for decades.

New Siding Benefits

Replacing or upgrading your siding isn’t just about aesthetics — it’s a smart investment in your home’s safety, comfort, and long-term value. Some of the key benefits include:

  • Improved Energy Efficiency: Modern siding materials provide better insulation and help lower energy costs.
  • Weather Resistance: Protects against rain, wind, snow, and UV rays to prevent rot and damage.
  • Structural Protection: Keeps moisture out, reducing the risk of mold, mildew, and structural decay.
  • Enhanced Curb Appeal: Instantly boost your home’s appearance and market value.
  • Low Maintenance: New siding requires less upkeep and stays cleaner longer.

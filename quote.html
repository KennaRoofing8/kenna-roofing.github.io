Our Easy 3-Step Quote Process

1

Schedule a free consultation visit at your home by calling us on the phone (203-824-3843), sending us an email (KennaRoofingLLC@yahoo.com) or by filling out the form below. Our expert team will work with you to find a time that fits your schedule and answer any questions you have before the visit.

2

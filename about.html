About Us

Meet the Owner

With over 29 years of being in business, owner Chris Leigh brings decades of experience in construction and home improvement to every project. His deep understanding of the trade, paired with a hands-on leadership style, ensures that every roof, repair, and renovation reflects the highest standards of quality. Chris believes that great work starts with great communication — taking the time to understand each customer’s needs and delivering solutions built to last.

Known for his positive attitude and unwavering dedication, Chris has built Kenna Roofing on a foundation of trust, integrity, and exceptional service. He takes pride in going above and beyond for his customers, treating every home as if it were his own. Under his leadership, Kenna Roofing continues to grow through honesty, craftsmanship, and a promise to deliver only the best.
